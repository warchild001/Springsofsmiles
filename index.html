About us

Springs of smiless miniistries(SSM) is a non-profit and a non-governmental organisation
that What was started by Ssebaggala Hassan on 07/July/2011. so far we currently have 65 Orphaned and vulnerable children under our care programme but the current promises which we are inadequate to

Caring for the young children of Africa most especially Uganda because its where we are located and aiming to help all children in needy on our universe so that they can acheive the basic needs and a healthy life we join our hands to give the the little we have to them

AIMS

Springs of smiles ministries are dedicated to demonstrating God's love by alleviating human needs and suffering in Kiryokya village and around Mityana district.
We're are also  currently seeking for good Samaritans to help us, lend to us and assist us in the building of the children's home, a clean water source (a borehole), a healthcare center among other structures to meet the children's needs as well as the community at large.

Explore our work

Partner with SSM and be a part of the life-changing activities we are doing to the vulnerable communities of Kiryokya village, Uganda East Africa.
We aim at improving the standards of living the young generation through providing the needs like fees,cloths,skolastic materials like books,pens,school bags & shoes. we also care about where the children are coming from through contracting new houses, village borewhole in order the make life enjoyeable for the children and elders in a given area.
